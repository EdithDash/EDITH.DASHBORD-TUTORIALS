E.D.I,T.H

Open your Discord server settings:Click on the server name in the top-left corner of Discord.
Select “Server Settings” from the dropdown menu.

Navigate to Roles:In the left sidebar, click on “Roles.”

Find the Bot Role:Scroll down to the list of roles.
Locate the role assigned to your bot. It should have the bot’s name or a similar identifier.

Adjust Role Position:The position of roles determines their hierarchy. Higher-positioned roles have more authority.
Drag the bot role to the top of the list (or as high as you want it to be).
This ensures that the bot’s permissions take precedence over other roles.

Save Changes:Click the “Save Changes” button to apply the modifications.
